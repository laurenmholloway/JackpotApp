Jackpot

Jackpot lets users generate random numbers for lottery game play.

Jackpot lets users generate random numbers for lottery game play. This app was made for entertainment purposes only. This app does not intend to replicate or simulate how winning numbers are drawn for the real lottery games. Current games included in the app are Powerball, Mega Millions, Pick 3, and Pick 4.
